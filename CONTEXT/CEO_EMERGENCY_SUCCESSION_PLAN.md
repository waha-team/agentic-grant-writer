# CEO Emergency Succession Plan

Source: `CONTEXT/Founding Documents.pdf` (rev date listed as Mar 28, 2025)

## Purpose

This plan is designed to preserve operational continuity if the CEO is suddenly deceased, incapacitated, or otherwise unable to serve.

## Immediate Interim Leadership

- Interim CEO: Josh Muller (pending Governing Board affirmation).
- If unavailable, the board appoints a qualified interim leader.

## First 72 Hours

The Governing Board convenes to:
- confirm interim leadership,
- review short-term priorities,
- establish communications and stabilization plans.

## Communication Plan

Interim CEO and Board Chair coordinate timely communication to:
- staff,
- board,
- major donors,
- strategic partners,
- and broader public audiences as needed.

## Operational Continuity Priorities

- Maintain core operations across technology, partnerships, development, and communications.
- Keep financial operations and donor relations uninterrupted.
- Continue current initiatives with minimal disruption.

## Board-Led Discernment for Permanent CEO

The board leads a prayerful and strategic succession process, which may include:
- input from the Strategic Advisory Team and Church Partnership Council,
- evaluation of internal and external candidates,
- a defined timeline for permanent succession.

## Ongoing Maintenance

- Review and update annually.
- Keep designated interim successor current and affirmed.
- Keep essential organizational documents and access credentials securely stored and accessible to appropriate emergency leaders.
