# Staff Policy Manual Summary

## Scope and Nature

- Handbook is informational and not an employment contract.
- Employment is at-will, subject to applicable law.

## Spiritual and Cultural Expectations

- Staff are expected to model Christian character and spiritual maturity.
- Active local church commitment is expected (Antioch affiliation encouraged where available, not required).
- Team culture emphasizes five relational resolves: honor, hard work, healthy conflict, honesty/vulnerability, relational warmth.

## Hiring and Employment Posture

- Faith-based hiring preference is asserted under religious-organization allowances.
- Equal dignity and lawful non-discrimination language included for protected classes.
- Contracts may only be signed with authorized approval.

## Role and Performance Management

- Written job descriptions at hire; periodic updates allowed.
- Semiannual performance reviews.
- Progressive correction process (informal -> written -> potential termination), with exceptions for severe cases.
- Standard resignation/exit expectations and transition process.

## Employment Classifications

- Full-time exempt (salary)
- Non-exempt hourly (overtime eligible)
- EOR international employees (subject to local law and EOR terms)

## Compensation and Time Rules

- Bi-monthly payroll cadence.
- Overtime rules for non-exempt staff under FLSA.
- Ministerial housing allowance framework with board oversight.
- Compensation review considers role scope, location cost-of-living, experience, and budget.

## Leave and Time Off (high-level)

- Vacation negotiated as part of compensation package.
- Sick leave, bereavement, jury duty, and approved unpaid leave frameworks included.
- Maternity/paternity/adoption leave included.
- Travel recovery days for qualifying exempt travel.
- Ministry Partner Development (MPD) days for qualifying support-raising roles.

## Workplace Standards and Safety

- Harassment, discrimination, and bullying prohibited.
- Conflict resolution follows direct-conversation escalation model.
- Weapons restrictions, controlled-substance restrictions, and distracted-driving expectations.

## Financial and Expense Governance

- Accountable reimbursement plan with timing and documentation requirements.
- Corporate card controls and restrictions.
- Business-meal and mileage reimbursement guidance.

## Data, Confidentiality, and Communications

- Confidentiality expectations for internal/donor/strategic information.
- Media communication routed through approved spokespersons.
- Brand standards and approved public-facing asset usage.

## Governance and Legal Safeguards

- Whistleblower protections and anti-retaliation posture.
- Document-retention framework (including permanent and multi-year categories).
- Intellectual property generally treated as work-for-hire.
- Likeness and background-check language included.
