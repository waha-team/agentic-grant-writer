## Mission

Waha empowers people everywhere to become disciples who multiply disciples among the unreached and unchurched.

## What We Do

Waha builds and distributes a free, ad-free mobile app that makes disciple-making simple and accessible for anyone. We have two core products:

### 1. Discovery Bible Study (DBS) App
- Audio-first Bible study lessons that anyone can facilitate — no pastor, no prep, no expertise needed
- "Gather, press play, discuss" format
- Hundreds of lessons across Foundations (e.g., "Creation to Christ," "Life and Message of Jesus"), Topics (faith, peace, parenting, grief, money, etc.), and advanced tracks
- Available on iOS, Android, and web
- Fully functional offline after initial download
- SD card distribution for areas without internet or smartphones
- Security mode (piano/calculator disguise) for restricted-access nations
- Data encrypted at rest and in transit, audited by CISSP/CISM/CEH certified consultant

### 2. Disciple Making Course (DMC)
- 9-week interactive training for believers to learn why and how to make disciples
- No preparation required — gather a group, press play
- Designed to produce multiplication: believers who train other believers who start their own groups
- Available as cohort-based training with coaching support

## What Makes Waha Distinctive

1. **Radical simplicity** — Anyone who can use Spotify can facilitate a Bible study. No training, no books, no leader preparation.
2. **Audio-first design** — Works for non-readers, low-literacy populations, and oral cultures.
3. **Security-conscious** — Built for restricted-access nations with disguise modes, encryption, and seeker-friendly design that hides strategic content.
4. **Offline-first** — Download once, use forever without internet. SD card distribution reaches the most remote villages.
5. **Accessibility** — ASL video lessons, full screen reader support, audio-first for non-readers.
6. **Multiplication model** — Not content consumption; designed to produce leaders who start their own groups. Documented cases of 3rd-generation multiplication.
7. **Last mile of Bible translation** — Waha takes partially or fully translated scriptures and puts them into a format that produces mature disciples, bridging the gap between translation completion and actual community impact.
