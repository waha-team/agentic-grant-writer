## Mission

Waha empowers people everywhere to become disciples who multiply disciples among the unreached and unchurched.

## The Problem We Solve

Our communities are increasingly unchurched, and there remain **7,000 unreached people groups** across the world. Jesus commissioned every believer to make disciples, but few know how. The gap isn't motivation — it's method.

Three specific problems:
1. **The training gap:** Most believers lack the tools and confidence to lead others in Scripture. They've been told to "go and make disciples" but never shown how.
2. **The last-mile gap:** Bible translations exist in hundreds of languages but aren't reaching people. There's no simple pathway from "translation completed" to "communities transformed."
3. **The access gap:** Billions of people live in oral cultures, restricted-access nations, or areas without reliable internet — making traditional discipleship methods impossible.

Waha exists to close all three gaps simultaneously.

## What We Do

Waha builds and distributes a free, ad-free mobile app that makes disciple-making simple and accessible for anyone. Two core products:

### 1. Discovery Bible Study (DBS) App
- Audio-first Bible study lessons anyone can facilitate — no pastor, no prep, no expertise
- "Gather, press play, discuss" format
- Hundreds of lessons: Foundations ("Creation to Christ," "Life and Message of Jesus"), Topics (faith, peace, parenting, grief, money), advanced tracks
- iOS, Android, and web
- Fully functional offline; SD card distribution for areas without internet
- Security mode (piano/calculator disguise) for restricted-access nations
- Encrypted at rest and in transit, audited by CISSP/CISM/CEH certified consultant

### 2. Disciple Making Course (DMC)
- 9-week interactive training: why and how to make disciples
- No preparation required — gather a group, press play
- Produces multiplication: believers train believers who start their own groups
- Also available as Disciple Making Challenge on YouVersion

## What Makes Waha Distinctive

1. **Radical simplicity** — "Anyone who can use Spotify can facilitate a Bible study"
2. **Audio-first** — Works for non-readers, low-literacy populations, oral cultures
3. **Security-conscious** — Disguise modes, encryption for restricted-access nations
4. **Offline-first** — Download once, use forever without internet
5. **Accessibility** — ASL video lessons, full screen reader support
6. **Multiplication model** — Produces leaders who produce leaders (documented 3rd-generation)
7. **Last mile of Bible translation** — Bridges translation to community impact
8. **Meta Language Technology Partner** — Recognized by Meta
