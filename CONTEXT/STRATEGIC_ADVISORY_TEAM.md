# Strategic Advisory Team (SAT)

Source: `CONTEXT/Founding Documents.pdf`

## Purpose

The Strategic Advisory Team helps keep Waha rooted in proven global Disciple Making Movement (DMM) principles and practices.

This team has advisory influence but no legal governance authority.

## SAT Role

- Advises: strategic input on product, content, and partnership decisions.
- Coaches: relational and spiritual feedback to sharpen executive leadership.
- Champions: represents Waha in networks, helps contextual expansion, and amplifies vision.

## Membership and Expectations

- Members are selected for demonstrated DMM fruitfulness, competence, and credibility.
- Term: 2-year renewable.
- Meeting rhythm: at least two virtual meetings per year, plus optional special initiatives.
- Expected posture: engaged, spiritually discerning, and aligned with Waha's disciple-making mission.

## Legal Clarification

The SAT has no legal authority, fiduciary responsibility, or voting power in Waha governance.
