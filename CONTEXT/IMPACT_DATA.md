## Case Studies

### 1: Church Leader Multiplication
DMC training with church leaders → 30 groups multiplied to 90 in 50 days. 20% were 3rd-generation groups. All reaching unchurched people.

### 2: Youth Group Doubling
Church used Waha in youth group → grew every week as kids invited friends → doubled in size. Students experienced "revelation of God, understanding of themselves, and a call to be on mission."

### 3: Short-Term Missions Transformation
Missionaries with years of failed models → introduced Waha DMC → shifted from dependency on long-term missionaries to empowering locals.

### 4: Iran / Farsi
Partnership with Iran Alive for Farsi translation. Extreme persecution context. Security features critical. Church in Iran growing rapidly.

### 5: Dubai Mission Trip
Used on mission trip to Dubai AND in US home churches. Demonstrates cross-cultural versatility.
