## Documented Outcomes (Case Studies)

### Case Study 1: Church Leader Multiplication
- DMC training with several church leaders
- Result: Multiplied from 30 groups to 90 total in 50 days
- 20% of new groups were 3rd-generation (groups started by groups started by original groups)
- All new groups were reaching unchurched people

### Case Study 2: Youth Group Doubling
- Church began using Waha in youth group
- Youth group grew every single week as kids invited friends
- Doubled in size
- Students experienced "revelation of God, understanding of themselves, and a call to be on mission"

### Case Study 3: Short-Term Missions Transformation
- Missionaries Lauren and John had years of failed short-term trip models
- Locals would pray with short-termers but refuse follow-up discipleship
- Introduced Waha Disciple Making Course to short-term teams
- Shifted from dependency on long-term missionaries to empowering locals

### Case Study 4: Iran / Farsi
- Partnership with Iran Alive to translate Waha into Farsi
- Designed for a context of extreme persecution
- Security features (encryption, disguise mode) critical for Iranian believers
- Church in Iran growing rapidly; Waha positioned to accelerate growth

### Case Study 5: Dubai Mission Trip
- Used on a mission trip to Dubai
- Same tool also used in US home church context
- Demonstrates cross-cultural versatility
