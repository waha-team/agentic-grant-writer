## Handling "No 990" in Applications

Some foundations require the most recent 990. For new nonprofits:

1. **Explain in application:** "Waha received 501(c)(3) status in 2025; our first 990 will be filed for the 2025 tax year."
2. **Provide alternative:** Offer board-approved budget, bank statements, or financial projections.
3. **Some foundations accept:** 501(c)(3) determination letter in lieu of 990 for new nonprofits.
