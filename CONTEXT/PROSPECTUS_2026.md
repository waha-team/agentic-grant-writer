## The Solution

**Waha empowers and equips people everywhere to multiply disciples and churches among the unchurched and unreached.**

The Waha app provides:
- **Discovery Bible Study facilitation** — structured group study sessions with fellowship questions (e.g., "Based on what has happened with you since the last time we met, what is something you are thankful for?"; "What has stressed you out this week, and what do you need for things to be better?"; "What are the needs of the people in our community, and how can we help?")
- **Audio-based curriculum** — lessons with play/pause controls (e.g., "How to Start Discovery Bible Studies with Seekers" — 90-minute session)
- **Content tabs:** Fellowship, Story, Application
- **Video integration:** YouTube lessons embedded in app
- The app is available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play)

## Our Programs

Waha operates under three program pillars:

### 1. Equip — Product
Equip disciple-makers with proven methodologies, apps, and tools to make church planting simple and scalable.

### 2. Empower — Mobilization
Inspire, train, and coach ordinary believers to rediscover their calling as disciple-makers, moving them from passive consumers to active multipliers.

### 3. Everywhere — Localization
Translate and localize discipleship content and training so multiplication can happen in every language and culture — including low-resource and unreached contexts.

## 3-Year Vision (Five Strategic Pillars)

### 1. Mobilize 250,000 Disciple-Makers *(Mobilization)*
Missions cannot scale if disciple-making depends on professionals. By activating 250,000 ordinary believers, Waha will catalyze **3,000+ multiplying churches worldwide**.

### 2. Build Infrastructure Through Key Hires *(Mission Support)*
To avoid burnout and strengthen Waha's capacity, planned hires include:
- Head of Product
- Head of Development
- Head of Marketing
- Seattle-based Administrative Assistant
- Additional Software Engineer

### 3. Reach Seekers and Mobilize Believers Online *(Mobilization)*
Millions of people search online every day for meaning, yet few are invited into reproducible discipleship. Through **15 strategic digital initiatives**, Waha will find seekers and mobilize believers — equipping both to multiply as disciples.

### 4. Scale Disciple-Making Coaching Worldwide with AI *(Product)*
There are not enough human coaches to keep up with practitioners seeing fruit. Waha will deploy an **AI-powered coach** to provide real-time guidance to seekers and emerging leaders at global scale.

### 5. Translate Waha into 100 Languages *(Localization)*
Seventy percent of the world belongs to oral cultures. If the content doesn't adapt to how people learn, disciple-making will not scale. Waha will translate its audio-based curriculum into **55 additional languages** (from current ~45+), bringing reproducible disciple-making to **100 languages worldwide**.
