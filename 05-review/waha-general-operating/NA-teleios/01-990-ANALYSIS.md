## First-Time vs. Repeat Grantee Analysis

- **Do they give to new organizations?** Yes — Teleios appears to fund startup ministries and small church networks with limited track records
- **Typical first-time grant amount:** $5,000–$14,000
- **Repeat grantee patterns:** Likely to fund same grantees multiple years based on mentorship relationship investment
- **New grantee consideration:** The 12-week mentorship for grants >$5,000 creates a relationship-building phase that may lead to ongoing support

**Strategic implication:** For Waha's first application, consider requesting $5,000 to avoid the mentorship requirement, OR accept the mentorship if seeking $14,000. Either approach is viable for a new grantee.
