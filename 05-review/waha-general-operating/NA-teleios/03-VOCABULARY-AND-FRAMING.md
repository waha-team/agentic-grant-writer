## Framing the "General Operating" Issue

Teleios requires 100% of grant funds for specific purpose, with max 15% on non-Bible-teaching/evangelism expenses ([Foundation Website — Grant Requirements](https://teleiosgrant.com/grant-requirements/)). This means pure general operating support is NOT compatible. Reframe as:

**"Fund Waha's DBS Evangelism Training Program — equipping believers worldwide to teach the Bible and share the plan of salvation through Discovery Bible Study."**

This is a genuine, specific program (the Mobilization/training pillar) and is 100% Bible teaching and evangelism in nature. It is not a misrepresentation — it is choosing the right slice of Waha's work for this funder.

**Budget framing:**
- Direct Bible teaching content (DBS curriculum and audio) = 85%+ of request
- Program administration and reporting = 15% max

## Proposal Structure (Per Teleios Workbook Format)

Teleios uses a concise Excel workbook with these fields (max 6 lines per cell):

1. **Grant Goal (one only):** "Equip 1,000 believers in 10+ countries to teach the Bible and share the plan of salvation through Discovery Bible Study groups, producing 200+ new DBS groups and documented professions of faith."

5. **How Success is Measured:** New DBS groups started; professions of faith documented; believers completing DMC training; multiplication to 2nd-generation groups.
