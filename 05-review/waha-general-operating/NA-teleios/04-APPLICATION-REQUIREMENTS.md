Teleios requires ([Foundation Website — Grant Requirements](https://teleiosgrant.com/grant-requirements/)):
- 85%+ of grant on Bible teaching/evangelism activities
- 15% max on non-evangelism expenses (administration, reporting)
- Grant tied to SPECIFIC project purpose — not general organizational budget
