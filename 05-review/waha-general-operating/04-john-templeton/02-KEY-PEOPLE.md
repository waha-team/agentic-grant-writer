## Academic Partner Requirement

**This is the most important "key person" issue for the Templeton application.**

Templeton funds research, not ministry delivery alone. The OFI and full proposal must name an academic partner — a university, seminary, or research institute — with:
- A named principal investigator (PI) with academic credentials
- A defined research methodology
- Capacity to produce peer-reviewed output

**Recommended academic partner categories for Waha's proposal:**

- **Missiologists / Church Growth researchers:** Scholars studying disciple-making movements, church growth in the Global South, and oral cultures. Baylor University's Institute for Studies of Religion (ISR) has funded Templeton relationships and studies church growth globally.

- **Oral culture / linguistics researchers:** Scholars studying how oral populations engage with Scripture differently from literate populations. Faith Comes By Hearing (FCBH) has produced research in this area; academic partners who have worked with FCBH could co-design a Waha study.

- **Digital religion scholars:** Researchers studying how mobile technology affects religious practice and spiritual formation. Syracuse University, Wheaton College, and Fuller Theological Seminary all have scholars in this space.

## Recommended Contact Strategy

**DIRECT APPLICATION via OFI system** — Templeton's process is specifically designed for unsolicited inquiries and does not require prior relationship.

**Recommended pre-OFI action:** Before submitting the OFI, identify and secure commitment from an academic partner. A Templeton program officer who reads an OFI without a named academic partner will immediately see a gap. The OFI is much stronger with "in partnership with [X University / Dr. Y]" in the project description.

**Step 1:** Josh engages Stan Parks about identifying a missiologist willing to co-design a research study on Waha's impact.

**Step 2:** Once academic partner is identified, draft OFI with: research question, methodology, partner name, budget, and connection to "Religion, Science, and Society" or "Character Virtue Development in the Digital Age."

**Step 3:** Submit OFI at https://www.templeton.org/grants/apply-for-grant (OFI system). OFI deadline cycles: typically August for the following January full proposal deadline. Check current calendar.

**Step 4:** If invited to full proposal, engage academic partner to develop full research design.
