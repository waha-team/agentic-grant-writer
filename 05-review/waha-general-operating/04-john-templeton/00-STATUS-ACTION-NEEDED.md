The John Templeton Foundation does not fund ministry delivery alone. Every successful proposal includes:
1. A named academic institution (university, seminary, or research institute)
2. A named principal investigator (PI) with academic credentials
3. A defined research methodology appropriate to peer-reviewed publication

Without a named academic partner and PI, the OFI will be declined at the first filter. This is not negotiable — it is the foundational structural requirement of any Templeton proposal.
