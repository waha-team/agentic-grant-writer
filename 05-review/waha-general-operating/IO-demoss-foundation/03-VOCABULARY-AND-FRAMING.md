*This file maps their language to ours. Note: the DeMoss Foundation is low-priority (operating foundation, spend-down trajectory, no unsolicited applications). This framing is for future use if a personal connection to the DeMoss family emerges.*

## Their Stated Values (from 990 mission statement)

1. Christian evangelisation and discipleship is the foundation's core purpose.
2. The entire world ("peoples of the world") is the geographic scope.
3. "By any and all proper means" — including technical assistance and literature.
4. Support for the full spectrum of Christian workers: pastors, evangelists, and missionaries.

---

## How We Map to Each Value

1. **Evangelisation and discipleship** → Waha's two products — the Discovery Bible Study app and the Disciple Making Course — are literally evangelisation (bringing seekers to Scripture for the first time) and discipleship (training believers to make more disciples). The mission statement is Waha.

2. **Peoples of the world** → 177+ countries, 46+ languages. The full 3-year vision: 100 languages by 2028, 250,000 disciple-makers. This is a serious global effort.

3. **By any and all proper means, including technical assistance** → Waha is technical assistance to missionaries. A free app designed for contexts where missionaries cannot openly operate is exactly "technical assistance by any proper means."

4. **Pastors, evangelists, missionaries** → Waha is used by Cru workers, YWAM teams, Pioneers missionaries, IMB field workers — the same community the DeMoss Foundation supports through Cru and Mission Eurasia.

## Framing Recommendations (for future use if connection emerges)
- **Lead with:** The Cru connection — "Waha partners with Cru, whom your foundation supports. Their missionaries use our platform in the communities they serve."
- **Nancy DeMoss Wolgemuth angle:** If approaching through Nancy, lead with women's discipleship — Waha has documented impact with women disciple-makers in the MENA region and restricted-access nations. The MENA TikTok house church story is a powerful example.
- **"Technical assistance to missionaries" language:** This exact phrase from the DeMoss mission statement is Waha's best framing hook. "The DeMoss Foundation was established to provide technical assistance to missionaries — Waha is technical assistance."
- **Tone:** Theologically serious, doctrinally grounded, biblically rooted. Nancy DeMoss Wolgemuth's world values theological depth over populist enthusiasm.
- **Funder type:** Conservative evangelical / Reformed-leaning. Use Scripture references sparingly but meaningfully.
