## Financial Trajectory
The foundation is drawing down its endowment ($2.6M revenue vs. $9.4M expenses = ~$6.8M annual drawdown). At this rate, the foundation has approximately 5-6 years of grantmaking capacity remaining at current levels, unless assets are replenished or expenses are reduced. This is significant context: the foundation may be in a spend-down phase.

**Implication for Waha:** Urgency of approach is higher, not lower. If the foundation is spending down, there is a limited window to establish a relationship and receive a grant.
