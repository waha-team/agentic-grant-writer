## Organizational Leadership

### John Ankerberg — Founder and Host, The John Ankerberg Show
- **Background:** Theologian, apologist, and broadcaster. Founded The John Ankerberg Show in the early 1980s. Has produced thousands of episodes on Christian apologetics, biblical doctrine, and comparative religion. Earned degrees in Christian Education and Theology. Author of numerous apologetics books.
- **Credibility:** One of the longest-running evangelical apologetics broadcasters in the country. Known for engaging with other worldviews, cults, and comparative religion.
- **Audience:** Large evangelical viewership on Christian TV networks and online. Audience skews toward doctrinally serious evangelicals interested in biblical accuracy and defense of the faith.
- **Connection to Bible access:** Has partnered with FCBH since 2013 to fund audio Bible distribution ([MinistryWatch](https://ministrywatch.com/amp/ankerberg-confirms-raising-20m-for-audio-bibles-but-kept-80-percent-for-production-air-time-other-expenses/)). Note: The ECFA terminated Ankerberg's membership in 2023 over concerns about how audio Bible funds were used — 80% of ~$20M raised was retained for production and airtime rather than passed to FCBH ([Christianity Today](https://www.christianitytoday.com/news/2023/august/ankerberg-financial-allegations-misuse-funds-audio-bibles.html)).
- **LinkedIn:** Limited public profile — search "John Ankerberg" for any connections.
- **Notes for engagement:** Ankerberg would need a personal connection to Waha leadership. A shared network contact (apologetics community, evangelical media, Bible access community) is the right entry point.
