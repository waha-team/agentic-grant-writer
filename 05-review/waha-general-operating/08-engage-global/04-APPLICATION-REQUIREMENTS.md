## Concept Note Template

*(1 page maximum; attach to initial email)*

**Opening:** Reference the Pioneers connection and shared unreached peoples focus.

**Problem:** Churches trained by Engage Global are motivated to engage unreached peoples — but when they send people, those believers often lack simple, culturally appropriate tools for leading others in Scripture. The last mile from training to multiplication is still missing.

**Solution:** Waha's free DBS app fills that gap — 46+ languages, offline, no expertise required. Supports all five habits Engage Global trains. Used daily by Pioneers missionaries they already fund.

**Ask:** $25,000 general operating support, specifically toward Waha's Mobilization program.

**Outcomes:** Specific to their frame — churches mobilized, unreached peoples engaged, disciple-makers multiplied.

## Key Framing Reminder

Use Engage Global vocabulary throughout: "mobilize churches," "unreached peoples," "Great Commission," "five habits," "partnering organization." Position Waha as a strategic partner serving the same mission — not as a project applicant. The Pioneers connection is the single most important credential to lead with.
