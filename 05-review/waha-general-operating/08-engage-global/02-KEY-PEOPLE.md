### RELATIONSHIP POSTURE

**Frame Waha as a strategic partner, not a grant applicant.**

Engage Global calls their grantees "Partnering Organizations." This is not donor-speak — it reflects how they view relationships. Lead with:
- Shared mission (unreached peoples, Great Commission)
- Complementary roles (they train churches; Waha equips the people they train)
- Mutual connection (Pioneers uses Waha)

**Avoid:**
- "Applying for a grant"
- "Submitting a proposal"
- "Seeking funding"

**Use instead:**
- "Exploring a partnership"
- "Seeing if there's a fit"
- "Waha as a resource for the churches you train"
