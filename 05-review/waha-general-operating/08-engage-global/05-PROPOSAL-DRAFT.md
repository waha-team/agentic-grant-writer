### Executive Summary

In 177+ countries, 82,000+ believers are using a free mobile app called Waha to facilitate Discovery Bible Studies — leading unreached peoples and unchurched neighbors through Scripture using a simple, reproducible method that requires no pastoral training, no preparation, and no literacy. Waha is the tool churches mobilized by Engage Global's training need when they go: something that works in any language, offline, and in any security context. We are seeking $25,000 in general operating support from Engage Global to fund our Mobilization program — the training and coaching infrastructure that equips disciple-makers worldwide to reach unreached peoples through Discovery Bible Study.

### Statement of Need: The Mobilization Gap

Rick Davis returned from Asia in 2011 with a clear conviction: churches needed to be equipped to reach the least-reached peoples of the world. Engage Global was born from that conviction. Over a decade later, Engage Global has trained thousands of believers in the five habits of a Global Christian — and those believers are ready to engage.

But there is a gap between motivation and multiplication.

When a church member goes — whether on a short-term trip, as a career missionary, or as a neighbor reaching out to internationals in their own city — they need more than conviction. They need a method. A tool. Something they can use with a group of seekers that doesn't require them to be a pastor, speak the local language fluently, or have a seminary degree.

Consider what is missing:

- **7,000 unreached people groups** worldwide have little to no access to the Gospel
- Bible translations exist for thousands of languages — but without a mechanism for community-based engagement, they remain on shelves (digital and physical)
- 83% of churchgoers have never been equipped to share their faith (Barna Research)
- The fastest-growing segment of global Christianity — national believers in the Global South — often lack resources in their own language to lead others in Scripture

The five habits Engage Global teaches produce church members who are praying, willing to go, and ready to welcome. What they often lack when they arrive is the simplest thing: a way to open Scripture with a group of seekers and let the Word do its work.

---

### Our Solution: Waha Supports All Five Habits

Waha is a free, audio-based mobile app that empowers any believer to lead Discovery Bible Studies in 46+ languages. The method: gather, press play, discuss. No preparation, no expertise, no internet required. Available on iOS, Android, and web; fully functional offline after initial download.

**How Waha supports every habit Engage Global trains:**

| The Habit | Waha's Role |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Pray** | DBS groups engage Scripture that produces intercessors; Waha makes God's Word accessible for prayer in every language |
| **Send** | Every believer Engage Global trains and sends needs a disciple-making tool — Waha is that tool, free and in their pocket |
| **Welcome** | Waha is available in diaspora heart languages — equipping Twin Cities churches to reach Somali, Hmong, or Arabic-speaking neighbors |
| **Mobilize** | Waha's 9-week Disciple Making Course trains any church member to mobilize more disciple-makers |
| **Go** | DBS gives every believer who goes a reproducible method that doesn't require them to be an expert |

**What this grant funds:**

$25,000 supports Waha's Mobilization program — the training, coaching, and regional coordination that equips believers in 177+ countries to lead DBS groups that engage unreached peoples. This includes:

- Cohort-based Disciple Making Course (DMC) training for emerging disciple-makers
- Regional coordinator support (covering Asia, East Asia, North America, Deaf community)
- Coaching for leaders whose DBS groups are beginning to multiply
- Partnership development with Engage Global's church network (enabling churches Engage Global trains to use Waha with their members)

### Evidence of Impact

The model works. Here is what Engage Global's church network can expect from mobilized believers using Waha:

**Philippines — From 30 to 90 DBS Groups in 50 Days**
Pastor Nathanial trained 12 local leaders who formed 30 DBS groups of seekers. After a 50-day campaign, groups tripled to 90 — reaching approximately 500 people. Members participated in two groups simultaneously: one as learner, one as leader. A woman formerly in sex work joined because: *"I would never have joined this group if there was a pastor involved."* The simplicity of DBS reached her where traditional church could not.

**Middle East — A Movement That Multiplied Without Missionaries**
A single believer's outreach, catalyzed by Waha DBS groups, sparked a multi-country house church movement across the Middle East and North Africa. Over 100 churches have been planted in that region — many multiplied to the 4th and 5th generation — without requiring direct missionary facilitation. The believer who started it used TikTok to connect with others reporting dreams of Jesus, directing them to download Waha. A movement catalyzed without a single short-term team.

**Sweden — A Dying Church Revived Through DBS**
Pastor Johan's congregation averaged 60+ years old and projected closure within 15 years. He discovered DBS, contacted Waha to request a Swedish translation, recruited local believers to translate, record, and launch. Within 3 months: 562 Discovery Bible Studies facilitated. The church transformed from decline to multiplication. *"Radical confidence that God is not done with Sweden."*
