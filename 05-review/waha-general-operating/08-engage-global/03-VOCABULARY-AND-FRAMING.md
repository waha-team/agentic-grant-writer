## Their Core Convictions (Mirror These)

Engage Global's website articulates seven beliefs — use these to show alignment:

1. **"The Bible is the story of God's plan to gather worshippers from all nations"**
   - Waha framing: "DBS reveals that story to every nation in their heart language"

2. **"The primary task of global missions is to establish healthy churches among every people group"**
   - Waha framing: "DBS groups become multiplying churches — not gatherings dependent on outside leadership"

3. **"The church should prioritize reaching unreached and unengaged people groups"**
   - Waha framing: "Waha focuses its localization pipeline on language groups where unreached peoples live"

4. **"The primary and sustaining motive for global missions is a passion for God's glory among the nations"**
   - Waha framing: "Every new language launch brings God's glory to another people group"

5. **"The lost will remain lost unless the church sends missionaries"**
   - Waha framing: "Every church trained through Engage Global needs tools to actually reach the unreached — Waha is that tool"

6. **"Every believer can have a part in the Great Commission"**
   - Waha framing: "Waha makes the Great Commission accessible to any believer — no seminary required"

7. **"The local church is central in God's plan"**
   - Waha framing: "DBS is designed to form and multiply local churches, not create dependency on foreign workers"

## The Five Habits Alignment (Use This Framing)

Engage Global teaches five ways to live as a Global Christian. Waha supports all five:

| Their Habit | How Waha Delivers It |
|-------------|----------------------|
| **Pray** | DBS groups engage Scripture that produces intercessors for unreached peoples |
| **Send** | Waha equips those who go with free, offline, multilingual disciple-making tools |
| **Welcome** | Waha is available in diaspora heart languages — equips churches to reach internationals in Minneapolis and beyond |
| **Mobilize** | Waha's Disciple Making Course is a 9-week mobilization training anyone can lead |
| **Go** | DBS gives every believer who goes a simple, reproducible method for making disciples without pastoral dependency |

**Use this framing explicitly:** "Waha supports every one of the five habits Engage Global trains churches to practice."

## General Operating Support Framing

**The key pitch:** Engage Global trains churches. Waha gives those churches' members a tool to actually go do it — in any language, offline, free.

Engage Global is fundamentally about mobilization. Waha is the resource that mobilized believers need in the field. Together, they form a complete cycle: Engage Global creates the motivation and understanding; Waha provides the method and material.

**Their grant language:** Engage Global describes its grantees as "Partnering Organizations." Frame Waha as a strategic partner in their mission — an organization that serves the same churches they train, equipping the believers those churches send.

**Pioneers bridge:** Both Engage Global and Waha are closely connected to Pioneers USA. Engage Global funds Pioneers ($26K–$33K/year) as a "Partnering Organization." Pioneers missionaries use Waha's DBS tools daily. Lead with this — it establishes credibility without cold outreach.

## Draft "Why We Fit" Paragraph (Gen Op Version)

> Engage Global mobilizes churches to engage unreached peoples through the five habits of a Global Christian. Waha equips the believers those churches send with free Discovery Bible Study tools in their heart language — tools that support every one of those five habits: praying through Scripture, going with a reproducible method, welcoming diaspora neighbors in their own language, and mobilizing more disciple-makers who train others. Together, we cover the full cycle from motivation to multiplication. Pioneers USA — an organization you've faithfully partnered with for years — uses Waha's DBS materials in the field every day. A gift to Waha's general operations is an investment in the tool that extends what Engage Global's training makes possible.

## Recommended Tone

- **Peer-to-peer, collegial.** Rick Davis founded Engage Global out of 30 years of field missions. He speaks the language. Don't over-explain the Great Commission — lead with alignment.
- **Concrete and outcomes-focused.** Reference specific results: 82,000 disciple-makers, 177 countries, Pioneers partnership.
- **Brief is better.** No formal application portal — they'll read a 1-page concept note. Concise wins.
- **Partnership posture.** Their grant purpose is "Partnering Organizations." Apply as a peer partner in the unreached peoples mission, not as a grant applicant seeking charity.
