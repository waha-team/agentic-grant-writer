| Factor | Analysis |

| **New partner uncertainty** | First-time grants may be smaller than repeat grants |

| **Pioneers benchmark** | Pioneers receives $26K–$33K/year; Waha should ask in similar range |

**Rationale:** $25,000 is:
- Within the range of grants Engage Global already makes
- Comparable to what they give Pioneers (a Waha partner)
- Large enough to be taken seriously
- Small enough to not stretch their capacity

## Application Process

Engage Global has **no formal grant portal** and **no published application deadlines**.

From their operating model:
- Relationship-based giving
- Grants described as "Partnering Organizations"
- Small board means decisions are fast and personal

**Recommended approach:** Direct email to Tom Jensen (info@engageglobal.org) with 1-page concept note. Phone follow-up to 612-770-5998.

## Key Findings Summary

1. **Concentrated giving:** Only 2 grantees in recent years — high bar for new partners
2. **Strategic partners:** Not "applicants" — must be framed as peer organizations
3. **Pioneers connection:** Engage Global funds Pioneers; Pioneers uses Waha — strongest warm path
4. **Lean leadership:** 3 decision-makers; personal relationship matters
5. **Right-sized ask:** $25,000 fits their grant range and partner expectations
