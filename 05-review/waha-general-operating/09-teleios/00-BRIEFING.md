## General Operating Support Challenge

Teleios has one specific constraint that affects the gen op framing:

> **"100% of funds must be used for specific grant purpose"**
> **"Non-Bible teaching/evangelism expenses must not exceed 15% of budget"**

This means general operating support in the broad sense (fund-the-whole-org) is NOT compatible with Teleios. Their grant must fund a specific, clearly-defined purpose where at least 85% of the grant goes to direct Bible teaching and evangelism activity.

**Solution:** Frame the ask NOT as "general operating support" but as a specific program — the mobilization training program or the localization program — with a clearly Bible-teaching/evangelistic purpose. The DBS curriculum IS Bible teaching. The DMC IS evangelism training. This framing is honest and compatible with their requirements.

**Recommended framing:** "Fund Waha's DBS evangelism training program: equipping 1,000 believers to teach the Bible and share the plan of salvation through Discovery Bible Study groups." This is true, specific, and meets their 85% Bible teaching/evangelism threshold.
