## Framing Recommendations (if/when relevant)
- **Lead with:** Wycliffe connection — "We partner with Wycliffe Bible Translators, whom you've supported." This is the most direct credibility bridge.
- **Roberta's angle:** Frame Waha's localization work as cultural engagement — each heart-language recording is an act of cultural respect and artistic expression of Scripture.
- **Howard's angle:** Biblical scholarship and the Discovery Bible Study methodology as intellectually serious engagement with Scripture.
- **Tone:** Intellectually serious, culturally aware, theologically Reformed-friendly. The Ahmansons are not populist — they appreciate depth and care.
- **Avoid:** Populist evangelical language. "App" as primary descriptor. Marketing speak.
