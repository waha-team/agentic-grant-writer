# Status: Not Applicable — Vermeer Charitable Foundation

## Why We Can't Apply

**Two independent disqualifying reasons:**

### 1. Geographic Restriction (Primary Disqualifier)
The Vermeer Charitable Foundation's grant policy explicitly states: "Grants are made to non-profit organizations, not individuals or endowments, **primarily in the Pella area and other communities from which our team members commute.**"

Waha is headquartered in Spokane, WA (virtual address). Waha has no employees in Iowa, no operational presence in central Iowa, and no organizational connection to the Vermeer employee community. Geographic eligibility is a threshold requirement — no amount of mission alignment can overcome it.

### 2. Invite-Only Process (Secondary Disqualifier)
Even for geographically eligible organizations, Vermeer Charitable Foundation requires organizations to submit a letter to receive login credentials for the application portal. Their Grantmakers.io profile confirms: the foundation "only makes contributions to preselected charitable organizations." Waha has no relationship with the Vermeer or Andringa family that could open this door.

### 3. Mission Mismatch (Confirming Factor)
The foundation's giving is concentrated in Christian Reformed Church denomination institutions (Calvin Theological Seminary, CRC World Missions), local Pella schools, civic projects, and employee-serving programs. Waha's evangelical, interdenominational, international discipleship technology mission does not match the foundation's historical giving pattern.

---

## Alternative Approach

**There is no realistic alternative path to this foundation.** The geographic restriction is categorical and insurmountable without a physical presence in Iowa.

However, if Waha ever develops a significant partnership with a Christian Reformed institution (e.g., Dordt University in Sioux Center, IA — a Vermeer grantee), that relationship could theoretically create a future path through that institution's existing Vermeer relationship. This is a multi-year, indirect possibility, not an actionable path today.

---

## Keep or Archive?

**Archive.** Remove from active prospect list. This foundation cannot fund Waha under any realistic scenario. Document the research here for reference but do not invest additional time.

**Final status:** NA — Not Applicable
