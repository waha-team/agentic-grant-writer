## Proposal Title

---

## Executive Summary

Waha requests $25,000–$50,000 from The Finishing Fund to localize our audio-based disciple-making platform into [X] languages spoken by unengaged, unreached people groups (UUPGs). Each language launch equips indigenous believers — ordinary people with no theological training — to lead Discovery Bible Studies in their own heart language, multiplying home churches without dependence on foreign missionaries or infrastructure.

Waha is currently deployed in 46+ languages across 177+ countries. With 69 languages in our pipeline, we are positioned to accelerate engagement among the remaining UUPGs — specifically those with no gospel witness and no Scripture access in their language.

---

## The Problem

The Finishing the Task movement estimates **189 UUPGs remain** — representing **5.7 million souls** with no evangelical witness, no church, and no Scripture in their heart language.

Traditional missionary deployment reaches some, but cannot scale fast enough. Indigenous believers within these communities have no tools in their own language. They cannot send themselves. They are waiting.

---

## Our Solution

Waha is audio-based disciple-making infrastructure for indigenous missionaries.

In each language, Waha provides:
- Audio Discovery Bible Studies (DBS) — the full disciple-making process on any phone
- Offline operation — no internet, no servers, no infrastructure required
- Security features — disguise mode and encrypted storage for closed-country deployment
- Zero literacy requirement — for oral-preference communities

Once a language is launched, indigenous believers can:
1. Gather 2-3 people
2. Press play
3. Discover Scripture together
4. Multiply — leading to new home churches in their own community

No foreign missionaries. No pastors. No infrastructure. Just ordinary believers finishing the task.

## Why Waha

**Indigenous-first:** We do not send missionaries. We equip the believers who are already there.

**Language-specific:** Each launch targets a specific people group — not a region, not a country. We finish the task one language at a time.

**Secure:** Waha operates in nations where overt missionary work is impossible. Disguise mode and offline operation mean believers in closed countries can use Waha without detection.

**Multiplying:** Every group trained starts another. Documented home church births from the Philippines to the Middle East to India.
