## Their Stated Values (from GFM website and public statements)

1. The Great Commission is not optional — every believer is called to participate.
2. Indigenous and national believers are the most effective agents of gospel advance in their own communities.
3. Speed and urgency: the task should be completed in this generation.
4. Kingdom investment: donors are partners in the mission, not just funders.
5. The 7,000 unreached — specifically the UUPGs — must hear the gospel before Jesus returns.

---

## How We Map to Each Value

1. **Great Commission mandate** → Waha trains every believer — ordinary people with no theological training — to make disciples through a simple audio DBS model. This is not a professional missionary tool; it is the Great Commission democratized.

2. **Indigenous believers as primary agents** → Waha is explicitly designed so national believers lead their own groups with no dependency on foreign workers. Once deployed in a heart language, Waha multiplies through indigenous leaders — which is exactly the Finishing Fund's theory of change.

3. **Speed and urgency** → Waha can localize into a new language in months, not years. With 69 languages in the pipeline, Waha is accelerating coverage across people groups faster than any other tool in the space.

4. **Kingdom investment** → Frame the Finishing Fund's investment as infrastructure: every dollar funds localization that multiplies indefinitely. A $50,000 investment in a language launch creates a permanent, zero-marginal-cost resource for that people group.

5. **UUPGs specifically** → Waha's pipeline includes dozens of languages spoken by peoples who are unreached or underreached. ## Framing Recommendations
- **Lead with:** The specific UUPG language launches in Waha's pipeline. Don't lead with the app — lead with the people group.
- **Frame Waha as:** Infrastructure for indigenous church planting movements — not a Western technology product deployed in "closed countries."
- **Avoid:** "App" as a first word. "Software." "Platform" is acceptable but second to describing the outcome (indigenous disciple-makers, multiplying churches, UUPGs reached).
- **Tone:** Movement language. Urgency. The Great Commission. Finishing what was started. These people are not moderate — they are urgency-driven.
- **Funder type:** Evangelical / Great Commission / UPG-focused. Maximum missionary language is appropriate.
