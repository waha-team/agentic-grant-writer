[JOSH: Replace this paragraph with a reference to your actual meeting.] Thank you for taking the time to meet with [me/us] on [DATE]. Your commitment to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ through the Foundation's sixty-seven years of faithful grantmaking is a legacy the Huston family should be proud of — and it was an honor to share how Waha's work aligns with exactly what Charles and Ruth Huston set out to fund in 1957.

We are writing to formally request $10,000 in support from the Huston Foundation's Protestant Evangelical Fund for Waha's 2026 general operations — specifically our media-communication, discipleship, and missionary support work bringing the Gospel to developing nations and unreached communities through audio technology.

Waha is a free, audio-guided Discovery Bible Study app and Disciple Making Course available in 46+ languages across 177+ countries. Every session presents the Biblical claims of Christ and gives participants — 86% of whom are seekers encountering Jesus for the first time — real opportunities to receive Christ as their personal Savior. The model is simple enough for anyone: gather a group, press play, discuss the Scripture, and respond. No pastor required. No preparation required. No reading ability required. And crucially: the disciple-makers Waha produces go on to train more disciple-makers — producing the kind of multiplication that the Great Commission envisions.

We were encouraged to learn that the Foundation supported Biblica's Reach4Life Project in 2023. Biblica is a direct partner of Waha — our Discovery Bible Study curriculum uses Biblica translations, and Biblica Senior Director Alan White has described Waha's growth as "remarkable." Your investment in Biblica and your investment in Waha are funding two parts of the same pipeline: Biblica translates Scripture, Waha puts it in people's hands in an audio format that works for oral learners, restricted-access nations, and anyone with a smartphone. We are literally the last mile of what you have already funded.

The Foundation's founding purpose — "funding ministries that have a strong Protestant, Christ-centered, Biblical-based foundation with an effective prayer ministry and unique calling by the Holy Spirit to promote evangelism and discipleship around the world" — is Waha's mission statement. We build the infrastructure that makes personal evangelism possible for ordinary believers in the hardest-to-reach places on earth.

We have enclosed our full proposal, 501(c)(3) determination letter, Statement of Faith affirming the Foundation's doctrinal statement, organizational budget, and all required documentation. We are grateful for the Foundation's consideration and pray that the Lord would confirm whether this partnership serves His purposes.
