### Organization Description

Waha is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 35-2894107, 501(c)(3) status approved 2025) that builds and distributes a free, audio-guided Discovery Bible Study app and Disciple Making Course — empowering ordinary believers to lead personal evangelism and discipleship in every language, culture, and country. Our app is available in 46+ languages across 177+ countries, used by 82,000+ disciple-makers with near-zero marketing spend.

### Project / Program Description

We request $10,000 in general operating support for Waha's three-pillar ministry: (1) **Equip** — our media-communication technology platform that distributes audio Scripture curriculum to 177+ countries; (2) **Empower** — our Disciple Making Course, training believers in personal evangelism and producing disciple-making movements in developing nations; and (3) **Everywhere** — our translation and audio production pipeline bringing Waha's DBS curriculum into 69 new languages.

### Gospel Presentation

Every Waha Discovery Bible Study session presents the Biblical claims of Jesus Christ and creates natural, unhurried opportunities for participants to receive Christ as their personal Savior. Eighty-six percent of Waha's users are seekers — people who are not yet followers of Jesus — encountering Scripture, often for the first time, through the audio-guided format. The Disciple Making Course specifically trains believers in personal evangelism: how to initiate Gospel conversations, start DBS groups with friends and neighbors, and make disciples who make more disciples. Every dollar of this grant funds active, intentional Gospel presentation to unreached and unchurched individuals worldwide.

### Statement of Need

Seven thousand unreached people groups remain without meaningful access to the Gospel. Approximately 70% of the world's population are oral learners — people who absorb information through listening rather than reading — making text-based Scripture inaccessible to billions. Millions more live in restricted-access nations where open Christian activity is illegal or life-threatening. Traditional discipleship methods cannot reach these populations. Waha was built specifically for these gaps: audio-first, offline-capable, security-hardened, and available in heart languages.

### Evidence of Impact

- Philippines: 12 trained believers grew 30 DBS groups to 90 in 50 days — 20% now in the 3rd generation
- Middle East: One woman's Waha-catalyzed outreach produced church planting movements in multiple nations, 4th and 5th generation
- Sweden: 562 Discovery Bible Studies facilitated in 3 months after Waha Swedish launch, reviving a congregation projected for closure
- Restricted-access nation: A former militant received Christ through a Waha group; security mode kept the app invisible on his device

### Organizational Credibility

Waha is endorsed by Biblica (a 2023 Huston Foundation grantee), Wycliffe Bible Translators, YouVersion, the 24:14 Movement, and leading missionaries worldwide. We partner with IMB, YWAM, Pioneers, Frontiers, Cru, and Operation Mobilization. Waha is a Meta Language Technology Partner. Our revenue is diversified across grants (33%), major donors (41%), midlevel supporters (16%), and grassroots giving (10%) — we do not depend on any single funding source.

### Doctrinal Statement

Waha affirms the following without reservation:

We believe in the Holy Trinity, consisting of God the Father, Jesus Christ the Son, and the Holy Spirit; in the Virgin birth of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, who was begotten of the Holy Spirit and is true God and true man; in the sinfulness of men and in Salvation through personal faith in Christ; in His bodily resurrection; in His present ministry of intercession; and in His personal return for His church; in the Holy Spirit as a person who proceeds from the Father and the Son, by whom every believer is regenerated, indwelt, empowered and sealed into the body of Christ which he believes; in the Holy Scriptures as inspired by the Holy Spirit of God.
