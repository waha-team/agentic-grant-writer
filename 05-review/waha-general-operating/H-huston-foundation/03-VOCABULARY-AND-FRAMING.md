"The primary interest of the Foundation is in funding ministries that have a strong Protestant, Christ-centered, Biblical-based foundation with an effective prayer ministry and unique calling by the Holy Spirit to promote evangelism and discipleship around the world." "By providing funds, technical assistance, collaboration, and networking on behalf of not-for-profit organizations, the Directors of the Foundation seek to encourage individual and Christ-centered development, which promotes personal evangelism and discipleship." ## How We Map to Each Value

1. **Protestant, Christ-centered, Biblical-based foundation with effective prayer ministry:** Waha's entire product is Scripture — every session is a guided encounter with the Bible. The Disciple Making Course is built on Jesus's own command to "make disciples" (Matthew 28:19-20). The app catalyzes prayer as groups encounter Scripture together and apply it to their lives.

2. **Building up the Body of Christ through programs where Biblical claims of Christ are presented:** Every Waha session presents the Biblical claims of Christ directly to participants — including seekers who may be encountering Jesus for the first time. The format (audio, discussion, application, prayer) produces genuine transformation, not just information. 86% of Waha's installs come from seekers, not already-believing users.

3. **Individual and Christ-centered development promoting personal evangelism and discipleship:** The Disciple Making Course trains believers in personal evangelism — how to start a Discovery Bible Study with a friend, neighbor, or coworker. Users who complete the DMC become disciple-makers themselves, producing 2nd, 3rd, 4th generation groups. This is the precise definition of multiplication.

4. **Spread the Gospel nationally and internationally:** 82,000+ installs in 177+ countries, 46+ languages — all with near-zero marketing spend. Waha is spreading the Gospel in places where traditional missions cannot reach: restricted-access nations, oral cultures, conflict zones, and communities where a foreign missionary's presence would endanger local believers.

5. **Active presentation of the Gospel with opportunities to receive Christ:** Every Waha DBS session is structured to bring participants to a decision point — understanding who Jesus is, what He claimed, and what it means to follow Him. 86% of installs are seekers. The structure creates repeated, natural opportunities for people to respond to the Gospel.

## Framing Recommendations

- **Lead with:** The Great Commission mandate and the evangelism/discipleship gap — "Jesus commanded every believer to make disciples. Most believers don't know how." Then pivot to the specific problem in developing nations: 7,000 unreached people groups, billions in oral cultures without accessible Scripture.
- **Center on:** Spreading the Gospel and personal evangelism — use these exact phrases. Frame Waha as a media-communication and distribution tool (their language) that enables personal evangelism at global scale.
- **Name the doctrine:** Reference the Trinity, the Biblical claims of Christ, the Gospel, salvation through faith. Don't be shy about explicitly evangelical language — this funder requires it. Affirm their doctrinal statement.
- **Use their categories:** Frame Waha's work under their Evangelical grantmaking categories: "Discipleship" (DMC training), "Protestant Evangelical Media-Communication" (app + audio production + translation), and "Protestant Evangelism" (missionary support, church planting catalysis).
- **Avoid:** Technical/startup language, jargon about "product-market fit," secular framing. Also avoid overtly political language — Waha is explicitly non-political and should remain so.
- **Tone:** Warm, personal, testimony-driven, grounded in Scripture. This is a family foundation whose founders were missionaries. They respond to authentic Gospel passion, not polished grant prose.
- **Funder type:** Evangelical — use Great Commission language, personal salvation, discipleship, missions. See OUR_ORGANIZATION.md framing guide.

This is a direct precedent for Waha's ask.

## Draft "Why We Fit" Paragraph (in their language)

Waha's ministry exists to fulfill exactly what the Huston Foundation has supported since 1957: spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ through media, translation, and distribution — with every session presenting the Biblical claims of Christ and giving participants opportunities to receive Him as personal savior. Through our audio-guided Discovery Bible Study curriculum, now available in 46+ languages across 177+ countries, ordinary believers are equipped to lead personal evangelism and discipleship in their own communities — in developing nations, restricted-access countries, and oral cultures where traditional missionary work cannot reach. Like Biblica's Reach4Life Project, which the Foundation supported in 2023, Waha delivers Scripture in an accessible, culturally relevant format that builds up the Body of Christ from the inside out — not through dependence on foreign workers, but by catalyzing indigenous disciple-making movements led by local believers responding to the leading of the Holy Spirit.
