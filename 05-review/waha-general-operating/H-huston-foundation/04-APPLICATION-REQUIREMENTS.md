## Step 1: Letter of Inquiry (LOI)

The foundation reviews LOIs before inviting full applications. The LOI must be submitted through the online portal at grantinterface.com.

**What the LOI should accomplish:**
- Demonstrate Waha's Protestant Evangelical Christian identity and theology
- Show active Gospel presentation with opportunities to receive Christ as personal savior
- Describe the specific project or program being funded
- Indicate the amount being requested
- Establish Waha's organizational credibility

**LOI content requirements (inferred from website):**
- Organization overview and history
- Statement describing the project/program
- Evidence of Gospel presentation in the work
- Amount requested
- Contact information

**Note:** Specific LOI word limits or question format is not publicly listed on the website. The portal may contain specific fields. This should be asked during the Nancy Hansen meeting.

## Grantmaking Restrictions (What They Will NOT Fund)

([Foundation Website — FAQ](https://www.hustonfoundation.org/faq.cfm))
- Secular programs outside of North America (Waha is evangelical — this restriction does not apply)
- Scholarship support to individuals
- Intermediate organizations distributing to beneficiaries of their own selection — **NOTE: Waha distributes directly to end users, not through intermediaries. This restriction does not apply.**
- Individual or group travel
- Endowment funds
- Tickets for events or advertising
- Fraternal organizations
- Political candidates or parties
- Political Action Committees
- Veterans organizations, labor groups, social clubs
- Coverage of continuing operating deficits
- Document publication costs
- Capital building / construction
- Multi-year grant funding — **NOTE: Each application must be for a single year**

## Grant Ask Amount

**Recommended:** $10,000

**Rationale:** Average evangelical grant in 2023 was $10,379 ([Huston Foundation 2023 Annual Report](https://www.hustonfoundation.org/uploads/hus_ar_2023.pdf)). For a first-time, non-Pennsylvania applicant with international missions scope, $10,000 is the calibrated first ask. It positions Waha in the mainstream of their grant distribution without overreaching. Comparable: Forge Leadership Network received $20,000 for general operating ([Huston Foundation 2023 Annual Report](https://www.hustonfoundation.org/uploads/hus_ar_2023.pdf)), but they likely have a warmer relationship with the board.
