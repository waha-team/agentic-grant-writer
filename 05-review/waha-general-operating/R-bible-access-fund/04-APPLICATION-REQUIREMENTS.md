# Application Requirements: Bible Access & Engagement Fund (SWGP)

*Critical Note: This is a Donor Advised Fund platform — NOT a traditional grant application.*

---

## Platform Listing Process

SWGP does not accept traditional grant applications. Instead:

1. **Organization is vetted** — SWGP staff evaluate alignment with their mission
2. **Organization is listed** — Approved organizations appear on the SWGP giving platform
3. **Donors direct funds** — Individuals with DAF accounts choose where to give
4. **SWGP distributes** — The DAF host (likely The Signatry) processes the grant

**This means:** The goal is platform listing, not a specific grant amount.

---

## Requirements for Getting Vetted/Listed

### Alignment Requirements
- Mission must address "closing the Bible Gap"
- Focus on Scripture delivery in audio, digital, or video formats
- Emphasis on "hardest-to-reach places" (restricted-access nations, oral cultures, unreached peoples)
- Innovation in delivery methodology
- Collaborative approach (not competitive with other Bible access organizations)

### Documentation Likely Required
- 501(c)(3) determination letter
- Mission statement and impact metrics
- Financial statements or Form 990
- List of current partnerships (YouVersion, Wycliffe, etc.)
- References from aligned organizations
- Description of security measures (if operating in restricted contexts)

### Vetting Criteria (inferred from website)
- Alignment with "Close the Bible Gap Alliance" values
- Demonstrated innovation and "righteous risk" approach
- Evidence of secure, contextual delivery methodology
- Contribution to the 2033 goal (every language having Scripture)
