## Summary

Waha requests to be vetted and listed on the SWGP Bible Access & Engagement Fund platform. ---

## The Problem We Solve

The Bible Gap is real and urgent. 

By 2033, every language will have some Scripture translated. But translation alone does not transform. Millions of believers in restricted-access nations hold translated Bibles they cannot read — oral cultures, underground churches, communities without schools or electricity. The gap between translation and transformation is the last mile of Bible access.

Waha closes that gap.

---

## What We Do

Waha delivers Scripture in audio format to the hardest-to-reach places:

- **46+ languages active** — with 69 more in localization
- **Audio-first delivery** — every lesson recorded by native speakers in heart language
- **Offline-capable** — works without internet, distributed via SD cards and solar players
- **Security-hardened** — CISSP/CISM certified encryption, disguise mode for persecuted believers
- **Contextual** — localized by native speakers with cultural adaptation
- **Community-based** — Discovery Bible Study format requires no pastor, no training, no infrastructure

Where YouVersion has put the Bible in 500 million hands, Waha activates it in communities that have never had access to Scripture in their heart language.

---

## Innovation & Righteous Risk

Waha was built by frontline workers taking righteous risks:

- **Security innovation** — We built disguise mode (app appears as calculator or piano) before it was standard practice, specifically for believers in restricted-access nations
- **Urgency innovation** — Our 7%-of-verses strategy launches languages with key Scripture passages rather than waiting for full translation, because believers cannot wait
- **Distribution innovation** — SD cards and solar players reach communities with no smartphones, no electricity, no connectivity
- **Cost innovation** — Audio production at scale means $2,000 launches a new language, reaching entire people groups

This is innovation in service of the 2033 goal.
