## Why They Might Say No

- **DAF structure:** Waha cannot "apply" in the traditional sense. The organization must be vetted and listed on the SWGP platform for donors to direct funds.
- **New initiative:** SWGP is relatively new. The platform may not yet have a large donor base or established grant volume.
- **Competition:** Other highly aligned organizations (illumiNations members, FCBH, YouVersion itself) are likely already on the platform.
- **Scale:** If the fund focuses on large-scale partners, Waha's size may require advocacy from a donor who specifically wants to support Waha.
