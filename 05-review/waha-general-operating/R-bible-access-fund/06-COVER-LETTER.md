Thank you for taking the time to meet with us on January 29. Our team left that conversation encouraged by the vision of the Bible Access & Engagement Fund and the collaborative approach SWGP brings to closing the Bible Gap.

I'm writing to follow up on our discussion and formally request that Waha be vetted for listing on the SWGP platform.

Since our meeting, we've continued to reflect on the alignment between SWGP's mission and Waha's work:

**Close the Bible Gap.** Waha exists to close the critical distance between "Scripture translated" and "community transformed." We serve the last mile of Bible access — delivering audio Scripture to communities that have translation but no way to engage with it.

**Hardest-to-reach places.** Our work focuses specifically on restricted-access nations, oral cultures, and communities without reliable internet or electricity. These are the places SWGP's mission names explicitly.

**Secure and contextual delivery.** Waha delivers Scripture in audio format with CISSP/CISM certified encryption and disguise mode for persecuted believers. Every lesson is recorded by native speakers in heart language.

**Innovation and righteous risk.** We built security features before they were standard practice. We launch languages with key Scripture passages rather than waiting for full translation, because believers cannot wait. We distribute via SD cards and solar players to reach communities with no smartphones.

**Collaboration.** We partner with YouVersion, Wycliffe, Biblica, and the 24:14 Coalition. Jenny Tangeman, YouVersion's Chief Strategy Officer, has publicly endorsed our work: "We want to partner with Waha because they are helping those in restricted access nations engage with the Bible."

We believe Waha is exactly the kind of problem-solver SWGP exists to lift up.

**Our request:** Please consider Waha for vetting and listing on the SWGP platform. We are prepared to submit any required documentation and would welcome a follow-up conversation to discuss next steps.

Thank you for your time and for the important work SWGP is doing to accelerate Scripture access globally. We look forward to continuing this conversation.

With gratitude,

Josh Müller
Executive Director
