## How We Map to Each Value

1. **Great Commission** → Waha's mission statement is a direct Great Commission mandate: "empowers people everywhere to become disciples who multiply disciples among the unreached and unchurched."

2. **Foreign evangelical missions** → Waha is U.S.-based (Spokane, WA) and serves 177+ countries. This is the trust's explicitly described ideal grantee: "global missions organizations located in the United States serving those areas."

3. **Africa, China, India, Latin America, Middle East** → Waha has confirmed users and impact stories in ALL FIVE of these regions:
   - **Africa:** Sub-Saharan Africa is among Waha's fastest-growing regions; multiple African language launches complete
   - **Middle East:** 100+ multiplying churches; MENA house church movement documented
   - **India:** Village transformation documented — persecution turned to mass baptism
   - **Latin America:** Castilian Spanish deployment serving Hispanic communities globally
   - **China/East Asia:** Aaron League (East Asia Regional Coordinator) active in region

4. **Christian education** → The Disciple Making Course is audio-based Christian education — 9-week curriculum teaching the biblical foundations of disciple-making, available in any language.

5. **U.S.-based global missions** → Waha is based in Spokane, WA (virtual office). 501(c)(3) established 2025. Fits the trust's "global missions organizations located in the United States" language exactly.

## Framing Recommendations
- **Lead with:** The Great Commission mandate — use their exact words in the opening paragraph
- **Highlight:** The geographic alignment — explicitly name Africa, China, India, Latin America, and Middle East with specific impact data from each region
- **Keep it simple:** This is a 3-page summary, not a full proposal. Write with clarity and conviction, not bureaucratic detail.
- **Avoid:** Tech-heavy language. The trust is mission-focused, not tech-focused. Lead with outcomes (disciples, churches, communities) and support with technology as the enabling tool.
- **Tone:** Direct, missional, Great Commission urgency. Harry Lloyd's spirit was generous and world-focused. Match that spirit.
- **Funder type:** Evangelical / Great Commission. Full missionary language is appropriate.
- **Do NOT mention:** Melanoma research (not relevant to Waha; risks confusion)
