## Page 1: The Great Commission — And a Tool to Fulfill It

Jesus commanded his disciples to go and make disciples of all nations. The Harry J. Lloyd Charitable Trust has faithfully supported that mandate for decades. Waha exists for the same purpose: to fulfill the Great Commission by equipping ordinary believers to make disciples in every language and nation.

**The Three Gaps**

Most Christians want to make disciples but face three barriers:

1. **Training gap** — Traditional disciple-making training requires literacy, internet, and weeks of classroom instruction. Billions of people have none of these.

2. **Last-mile gap** — Trained believers return to communities with no Scripture in their heart language. They have the knowledge but no tools.

3. **Access gap** — In restricted regions, believers cannot gather openly. They need something simple, discreet, and reproducible.

**The Waha Solution**

Waha closes all three gaps with one simple model: **Gather, press play, discuss.**

A small group gathers. Someone presses play on the Waha app. A Discovery Bible Study plays in their heart language. They discuss what they heard. They pray. They obey. They share with others.

No literacy required. No internet after download. No formal leader. Just the Word of God, in the language of the heart, available to anyone.

Waha provides:
- **Audio-based Discovery Bible Studies** in 46+ languages
- **Disciple Making Course** — a 9-week audio training on biblical foundations
- **Offline capability** — works without internet after download
- **Simple reproduction** — anyone can start a group, anywhere

The result: ordinary believers making disciples in places traditional missions cannot reach.

## Page 2: Our Impact in Your Focus Regions

The Harry J. Lloyd Charitable Trust focuses on Africa, China, India, Latin America, and the Middle East. Waha is active in all five.

**Middle East — Multiplication in the Hardest Places**

In the Middle East, over 100 multiplying churches have been launched through Waha. Many have reached 4th and 5th generation disciples — the disciples making disciples making disciples. One house church network started with a single Waha group and now spans multiple cities. These are believers who came to faith through audio Scripture in Arabic and Farsi, gathered in homes, and multiplied without formal church structures.

**India — Village Transformation**

In India, Waha documented a village that persecuted believers — then came to faith through Waha-facilitated Discovery Bible Study. A small group started meeting. Neighbors noticed. Opposition turned to curiosity. Curiosity turned to conversion. Today, that village has multiple home groups and a growing body of believers who once opposed them.

**Africa — Heart Languages for the First Time**

Sub-Saharan Africa is among Waha's fastest-growing regions. Multiple African language launches are complete, including languages where Scripture has never been heard in audio form. In one country, a church planter reported starting 47 new groups in six months using Waha in a local language. The barrier was not desire — it was access. Waha gave them access.

**Latin America — Spanish Deployment**

Castilian Spanish is one of Waha's core languages, serving Hispanic communities across Latin America and beyond. Missionaries in Mexico, Guatemala, and Colombia are using Waha to train believers who then start groups in their neighborhoods and workplaces.

**China / East Asia — Restricted Access, Open Impact**

Waha's East Asia Regional Coordinator is actively deploying the app in restricted-access contexts. Where believers cannot gather publicly, audio-based discipleship in small groups has become a primary strategy. We cannot share specific details for security reasons — but the work is real, growing, and multiplying.

**By the Numbers**

- **82,000+** users trained through the Disciple Making Course
- **177+** countries with active users
- **46+** languages launched
- **700+** known multiplying churches and house groups

## Page 3: Who We Are and What We Request

**About Waha**

Waha is a U.S.-based global missions organization — headquartered in Spokane, Washington. We received our 501(c)(3) status in 2025. Our team of ~12 staff serves 177+ countries through audio-based disciple-making.

We are not a traditional mission agency. We do not send missionaries. We equip the missionaries who are already there — national believers, indigenous leaders, and ordinary Christians in every nation.

**Our Model**

Waha provides the tool. Local believers provide the obedience.

- **Disciple Making Course** — audio-based training in the biblical foundations of making disciples
- **Discovery Bible Studies** — audio Scripture and discussion guides in 46+ languages
- **Global deployment** — free app, available worldwide, works offline
- **Multiplication tracking** — we follow up with users and document church multiplication

**Our Ask**

We request **$15,000 – $25,000** to support Waha's general operations — specifically, the expansion of our language pipeline and disciple-maker training in your five focus regions.

These funds would allow us to:
- Launch 2-3 new languages in Africa, India, or the Middle East
- Train 1,000+ additional disciple-makers through the Disciple Making Course
- Expand our follow-up and multiplication documentation in under-resourced regions

**How We Will Report**

We commit to providing an annual impact update including:
- Number of new language launches
- Disciple-maker training completion rates
- Documented church multiplication stories and statistics
- Geographic breakdown of new users in your focus regions
