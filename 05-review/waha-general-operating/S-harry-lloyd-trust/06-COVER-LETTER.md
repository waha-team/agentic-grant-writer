# Cover Letter: The Harry J. Lloyd Charitable Trust

---

Dear Cheryl Cole and the Trustees of the Harry J. Lloyd Charitable Trust,

The Great Commission — to go and make disciples of all nations — is the foundation of your giving, and it is the foundation of our work. I am writing to introduce Waha and to request your partnership in equipping ordinary believers across Africa, the Middle East, India, Latin America, and East Asia.

**Who We Are**

Waha is a U.S.-based global missions organization headquartered in Spokane, Washington. We equip believers to make disciples through audio-based Discovery Bible Studies in 46+ languages. Our model is simple: gather, press play, discuss. No literacy required. No internet after download. No formal leader training needed. Just the Word of God in the language of the heart.

**What We've Seen**

In the Middle East, over 100 multiplying churches have been launched through Waha. In India, entire villages have come to faith through Waha-facilitated home groups. In Sub-Saharan Africa, believers are hearing Scripture in their heart languages for the first time — and starting groups that multiply. These are the outcomes of foreign evangelical missions, and they are exactly what the Harry J. Lloyd Charitable Trust was built to support.
