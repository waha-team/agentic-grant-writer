## Required Content (inferred from trust's stated priorities)

Based on mission and focus areas, the 3-page summary should address:

1. **Mission alignment** — How does your organization fulfill the Great Commission through foreign evangelical missions?

2. **Geographic focus** — What is your work in Africa, China, India, Latin America, and/or the Middle East?

3. **Organizational overview** — Who are you? Where are you based? What is your legal status?

4. **Specific request** — What amount are you requesting? What will it fund?

5. **Impact evidence** — What outcomes can you demonstrate? Stories and data.

6. **Contact information** — Name, email, phone for follow-up.

## What They Do NOT Require

- No formal application form
- No online portal registration
- No letters of inquiry (LOI) or pre-approval
- No specific budget template
- No audited financials (not mentioned in public sources)
- No board list or staff bios (not mentioned)
- No specific reporting commitments (discuss post-award)

This is one of the most accessible application processes in this research set.

---

## Recommended Submission Approach

1. **Verify active status FIRST** — Call Cheryl Cole at 913-851-2174 or download the most recent 990-PF from ProPublica to confirm the trust is actively grantmaking.

2. **Write a 3-page summary** — Use the framing in 03-VOCABULARY-AND-FRAMING.md. Lead with Great Commission alignment. Include specific impact from all five focus regions.

3. **Send as PDF** — Email to LTrust@LTrust.org with a brief cover email.

4. **Follow up in 4-6 weeks** — If no response, a polite phone call to Cheryl Cole is appropriate.

## Ask Amount

**Recommended first-time ask: $10,000 – $25,000**

Rationale:
- Historical range: $5,000 – $400,000
- 42 grants in 2022 suggests portfolio approach with varying sizes
- Given uncertainty about current giving level, start conservatively
- If relationship develops, larger requests may be appropriate
