## Grant Ask Amount

**$15,000–$25,000** for general operations

- Lower end ($15,000) aligns with COTW median grant size and is appropriate for a first-time relationship
- Upper end ($25,000) aligns with their Timothy Initiative grant ([Cause IQ — Gospel Volunteers](https://www.causeiq.com/organizations/gospel-volunteers,596001191/)) and is appropriate if warm relationship is established first
- Recommend: **Ask $20,000** as the opening ask, with an explanation that it represents partial support for Waha's mobilization program (the pillar most aligned with COTW's "develop Christian leaders" value)

## Key Framing Reminder

Use COTW vocabulary throughout: "Biblical Truths," "global evangelism," "strengthen faith," "develop Christian leaders," "gospel impact." Lead with the existing Pioneers connection. Frame Waha as the tool that makes every missionary COTW funds more effective — not as a new or unfamiliar organization asking for money.

## Process After Submission

1. Submit Jotform application
2. Immediately email Tim T. (timt@cotw.org): brief intro, confirmation of application, mention of Pioneers partnership and Ann Baur connection, request brief follow-up call
3. Prepare for Missions Committee reference checks — Pioneers contact and at least one other strong reference
4. Prepare for possible interview with Missions Committee
