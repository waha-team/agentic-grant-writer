Dear Ann, Tim, and the Camp of the Woods Missions Committee,

I am writing to introduce Waha and to express our interest in supporting Camp of the Woods' global evangelism mission through a grant partnership.

You may already be familiar with us through Pioneers USA — one of your longtime mission partners and one of ours. Pioneers missionaries worldwide carry Waha's Discovery Bible Study tools into the field daily, using them to present Biblical Truths to unreached peoples and develop local Christian leaders who multiply disciple-making communities without requiring ongoing missionary presence. Ann, your work with Pioneers and your service on COTW's board represent exactly the kind of bridge we are hoping to build — a connection between organizations already aligned around the same Great Commission vision.

Waha is a free, audio-based mobile app and training platform that equips ordinary believers to lead Discovery Bible Studies in their heart language. No pastor. No prep. No seminary degree. Just: gather, press play, discuss. Since launching, we have equipped 82,000+ disciple-makers across 177+ countries, across 46+ languages — every one of them through word of mouth, with near-zero marketing spend. In the Philippines, 30 DBS groups multiplied to 90 in 50 days. In the Middle East, a single believer's outreach sparked a movement of house churches across multiple countries. In Sweden, a dying congregation launched 562 Discovery Bible Studies in 3 months.

What we do is not separate from what COTW's mission partners do — it is the tool they carry. Pioneers and Africa Inland Mission use Discovery Bible Study methodology to plant churches among unreached peoples. The Timothy Initiative, which you have generously supported, uses DBS to develop Christian leaders and advance global evangelism. Waha is the digital infrastructure that delivers DBS in any language, offline, and securely — including in restricted-access nations where traditional ministry is dangerous.

We are requesting $20,000 in general operating support for Waha's 2026 operations, with a specific emphasis on our Mobilization program — the training, coaching, and regional coordination that equips believers in 177+ countries to lead DBS groups that strengthen faith and develop leaders. This is the program pillar most directly aligned with Camp of the Woods' vision for global evangelism through leader development.

Enclosed is our full project application. We welcome the opportunity to speak with your Missions Committee and to provide references from our partners at Pioneers, Biblica, and YouVersion. We are grateful for your 125 years of faithful "vacation with purpose" and for the global evangelism legacy you have built — and we would be honored to be part of it.
