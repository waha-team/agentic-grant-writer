# 990 Analysis: The Varney Foundation

*Source: Grantmakers.io, ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer*
*990s reviewed: 2023, 2022, 2021*

---

## Giving Overview

- **Total grants per year:** $238,993 (2023); ~$27,000 (2022 direct grants); ~$24,200 (2021) ([ProPublica 990](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/541741120))
- **Number of grants per year:** 7 (2023)
- **Note on 2023 anomaly:** The $205,005 grant to National Christian Foundation is a DAF contribution and should be treated separately. Direct ministry grants in 2023 total ~$33,988.
- **Median direct grant size:** ~$1,500 (excluding NCF DAF and Cru multi-grants)
- **Average direct grant size (excluding NCF):** ~$4,800
- **Grant range:** $650 – $28,938 (direct ministry grants, 2023)
- **Most common grant size bracket:** $1,500 – $25,000

---

## First-Time vs. Repeat Grantee Analysis

- **Do they give to new organizations?** No evidence — all visible grantees appear to be multi-year recipients
- **Core/repeat grantees:**
  - Cru / Campus Crusade for Christ — received in 2023, 2022, 2021 (appears every year)
  - Wycliffe Bible Translators — received in 2023, 2022
  - Worldwide Discipleship Association — received in 2023, 2022

**Pattern conclusion:** This is a preselected, loyalty-based grantmaker. No evidence of first-time grantees being added. The Varney family gives to organizations they have trusted relationships with, likely built over many years.

---

## Geographic Patterns

- **Where do their grantees operate?** U.S.-based headquarters, international ministry scope
  - Cru: Orlando, FL (global operations)
  - Campus Crusade for Christ International: Orlando, FL (global)
  - Wycliffe Bible Translators: Orlando, FL (global — Bible translation)
  - Worldwide Discipleship Association: West Allis, WI (U.S.-focused)
  - Reston Bible Church: Sterling, VA (local)
  - National Christian Foundation: Alpharetta, GA (DAF platform)

**Geographic note:** No geographic restriction apparent. The Varneys fund international missions freely.
