## What They Actually Fund (from 990 analysis)

The foundation's 2023 990-PF reveals **7 grants totaling $238,993**:

| Grantee | Amount | Purpose |
|---------|--------|---------|
| National Christian Foundation | $205,005 | General Operations |
| Cru (Campus Crusade for Christ) | $28,938 | General Operating |
| Campus Crusade for Christ International | $24,000 | General Operating |
| Campus Crusade for Christ | $16,800 | Support of Public Charity |
| Worldwide Discipleship Association | $1,500 | General Operating |
| Wycliffe Bible Translators | $1,500 | General Operating |
| Reston Bible Church | $650 | General Operations |

**Key observation:** The $205K grant to National Christian Foundation (NCF) likely represents a donor-advised fund (DAF) contribution rather than a direct ministry grant. The "real" grantmaking is the six direct grants totaling $33,988, with Cru ($28,938 combined across multiple entries + additional) as the dominant recipient.

**2022 grants** show the same pattern: Campus Crusade for Christ International ($24K), Wycliffe Bible Translators ($1.5K), Worldwide Discipleship Association ($1.5K).

**Pattern:** This is a deeply loyal funder of Cru/Campus Crusade specifically. Cru has clearly been in their portfolio for years. Wycliffe and Worldwide Discipleship Association are secondary, consistent recipients. These are not new relationships — they represent multi-decade giving to preselected organizations.

## History & Context

The Varney Foundation is a small private family foundation controlled by Robert C. Varney (President) and Sandra D. Varney (Director/Secretary), with Tammy Boyd and Karlayne Powars as additional directors. Located in Great Falls, VA — a wealthy suburb of Washington D.C. — the foundation appears to be a personal philanthropic vehicle for the Varney family's evangelical giving.

Assets were listed at $716 as of 2023, suggesting the foundation distributes nearly all contributions in the year received (essentially a flow-through structure, likely funded by the NCF DAF or personal contributions annually). Total distributions in 2023: $239K.

The foundation is not publicly known, has no website, and gives entirely to organizations the family already knows and trusts. This is a relationship-based, personal grantmaker.
