## Their Stated Values (inferred from grantee selection)

1. **The Great Commission is the central calling of the church** — giving to Cru, which exists entirely for Great Commission fulfillment
2. **Scripture access matters** — Wycliffe giving demonstrates commitment to Bible translation
3. **Discipleship training equips the church** — Worldwide Discipleship Association giving
4. **General organizational support over project grants** — all grants are labeled "General Operating" or "General Operations"

---

## How We Map to Each Value

1. **Great Commission** → Waha equips ordinary believers in 46+ languages to make disciples who make disciples. "We have seen over 100 churches start because of Waha. Many of these churches have multiplied to the 4th and 5th generation!" — Maya, Muslim Background Believer in the Middle East.
2. **Scripture access** → Waha bridges Wycliffe's translation work to real communities: "Waha is exactly the kind of Bible engagement tool we want to use on the field" — Richard Hoyle, Wycliffe Bible Translators. Waha serves as the "last mile" — putting translated Scripture into practice.
3. **Discipleship training** → Waha's Disciple Making Course trains ordinary believers to lead Discovery Bible Studies, exactly as Worldwide Discipleship Association's model does — but in any language, anywhere, free.
4. **General operating support** → Waha's ask should always be framed as general operating support, matching the Varney pattern exactly. Do not reframe as a project grant.

---

## Framing Recommendations

- **Lead with:** The Great Commission mandate and Waha's unique position at the intersection of Cru's mobilization model and Wycliffe's translation work
- **Avoid:** Technology-forward framing; "app" language; secular development framing
- **Tone:** Personal, relational. This is a family foundation. Write like a letter to a fellow Great Commission investor, not a formal grant proposal.
- **Funder type:** Evangelical — lead with discipleship, Great Commission, multiplication
- **Key bridge:** "Waha does for Bible access what Cru does for campus ministry — equips ordinary believers to share their faith and make disciples, at scale, in every language."
