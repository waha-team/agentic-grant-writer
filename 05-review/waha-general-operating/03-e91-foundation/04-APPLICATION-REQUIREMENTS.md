## Step 1: Letter of Inquiry (LOI)

**Format:** "A brief introduction requiring less detail than a full proposal."
**Invitation needed:** NO — unsolicited LOIs accepted
**Specific instructions from FAQ:** The LOI requires "less detail than a full proposal" and no "specific funding amount required" at this stage.

**Best practice (inferred from grant patterns):** LOI should be approximately 1–2 pages and include:

- [ ] Brief organizational description (3–5 sentences)
- [ ] Description of the specific NEW or EXPANDING ministry initiative being funded
- [ ] Clear statement of why this is "new" or "expanding" (not existing operations)
- [ ] Geographic scope and people group being reached
- [ ] Approximate grant amount requested (or a range)
- [ ] Why this fits E91's stated priorities (use their language: "making disciples," "evangelism," "missions")
- [ ] Organizational tax-exempt status (501(c)(3)) and EIN
- [ ] Brief statement affirming Christian beliefs (Jesus's divinity and resurrection)

**What NOT to include in LOI:** Detailed budgets, lengthy organizational history, operational metrics that make Waha sound like an established institution. Keep the LOI focused entirely on the NEW initiative.

## Step 2: Full Proposal (by invitation after LOI review)

Full proposal requirements (from FAQ — "more detailed"):

### Narrative Components (anticipated)

- [ ] **Ministry Description** — Detailed description of the specific new initiative (Amharic DBS launch or AI coaching pilot): what it is, who it serves, how it works

- [ ] **Outcomes** — What will be different because of this grant? How will you measure it?
