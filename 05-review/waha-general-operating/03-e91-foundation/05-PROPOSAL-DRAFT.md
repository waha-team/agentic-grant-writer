### Executive Summary

There are 340 million people who speak Amharic — the official language of Ethiopia, the heart language of the Ethiopian diaspora worldwide. Of the 7,000 unreached people groups on earth, several thousand are clustered across the Horn of Africa and its diaspora communities. And until now, not one of them has had access to audio-guided Scripture discovery in Amharic — a simple, mobile-based resource any believer could use to make disciples without a pastor, without training, without internet.

That changes with this grant.

Waha is launching the first-ever Amharic Discovery Bible Study — a complete audio curriculum that equips any Amharic-speaking believer to lead Scripture discovery conversations in their family, neighborhood, or workplace. We are requesting **$5,000** toward the production of this resource — a one-time investment that will produce a permanent disciple-making tool available to 340 million people, free, forever.

This is exactly the kind of new ground E91 exists to plow.

### Our Solution: The First Amharic Discovery Bible Study

**What Waha does:**

Waha builds free, audio-based Discovery Bible Study content that any believer can use to facilitate Scripture discovery — no training, no preparation, no expertise required. The model is simple: gather a group, press play, discuss. Audio-guided lessons walk participants through Scripture stories, pause for discussion questions, and send groups out to apply what they've learned to their lives.

The platform works offline (critical for Ethiopia's rural communities), includes a security mode (the app can disguise itself as a calculator for believers in restricted areas), and is available at zero cost to users on iOS, Android, and web. For communities without smartphones, we deploy content via SD card and solar-powered audio players.

**What this grant specifically funds:**

The $5,000 grant toward the Amharic DBS launch would fund:

| Item | Amount |
|---|---|
| Audio recording production (native Amharic voice actors and studio time) | $3,000 |
| Bible licensing for Amharic Scripture passages used in curriculum | $1,500 |
| Quality assurance and testing with Amharic-speaking beta users | $500 |
| **TOTAL** | **$5,000** |

This is a one-time investment. Once recorded and licensed, the Amharic curriculum is available permanently, at zero additional cost, to every Amharic speaker on earth.

**How the multiplication works:**

One Amharic believer downloads Waha and leads a group. Each participant in that group is simultaneously a learner in one group and a facilitator leading their own group. Within weeks, groups multiply to second and third generations. In the Philippines, using exactly this model, 30 groups became 90 in 50 days — reaching over 500 unchurched people, with a built-in multiplication engine that continued without any outside involvement. We expect to see the same pattern in Amharic.
