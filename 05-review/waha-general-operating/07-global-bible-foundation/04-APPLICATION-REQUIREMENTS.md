## Stage 1 Inquiry Template

**Form field: "Describe your ministry and specific resource needs"**

*Suggested text (250 words or less):*

> Waha is a free, audio-based Discovery Bible Study app used by 82,000+ believers in 177+ countries. We exist to put God's Word into the ears of oral learners, restricted-access believers, and communities without Scripture in their heart language — audio-first, offline-capable, and always free. We currently operate in 46+ languages with 69 more in production.
>
> Our mission aligns closely with your current partners: like Oasis International, we bring Scripture to African language communities in accessible formats; like WordSpread, we equip believers in evangelism and discipleship through the Disciple Making Course. Where your partners deliver the written Word, Waha delivers the heard Word — for the estimated 70% of the global population who learn primarily by listening.
>
> We're reaching out because we believe Global Bible Foundation and Waha serve the same communities through complementary approaches. We'd love to explore whether a partnership could extend your reach to oral learner populations across Africa and beyond.
>
> Specific resource need: We're seeking partnership support for our audio Bible curriculum production and language localization — the work of translating, recording, and deploying Scripture-based Discovery Bible Studies in new languages.

## Grant Ask Amount

**To be determined after Stage 1 inquiry.** Without 990 data, GBF's grantmaking capacity is unknown.

**Preliminary range:** $5,000–$25,000 (for one or two language localizations or general operating contribution), sized down from typical general operating asks given GBF's apparent small scale.

**Alternative structure:** GBF may prefer to designate specific donor funds toward Waha rather than making a direct institutional grant. Be open to this model — it's consistent with how GBF operates.
